UX Research

Understanding the user.

By Luke Vincent Samson

Embarking on a journey of professional growth and learning, I recently had the incredible opportunity to intern at Andale, a dynamic startup revolutionizing the vehicle rental industry. My role as an intern was centered around UX research, an integral part of shaping user experiences. In this blog post, I'll be sharing my transformative experience and the invaluable insights I gained during my time at Andale. As an intern, my focus was on understanding the user experience from the ground up. I delved into the world of UX research, learning how to conduct surveys, interviews, and usability tests. These activities allowed me to comprehend the pain points and needs of users when it came to vehicle rental services. One of the highlights of my internship was the mentorship I received from seasoned UX researchers at Andale. Their guidance and insights were instrumental in helping me grasp the nuances of UX research methodologies and the art of deriving actionable insights from data. My time as an intern at Andale has been nothing short of transformative. From learning the ropes of UX research to contributing to the enhancement of user experiences in the vehicle rental domain, every moment has been a stepping stone towards personal and professional growth. As I move forward, I carry with me a deeper understanding of the intricacies of user-centric design and an unwavering enthusiasm to continue making a positive impact in the world of technology and user experiences.

Author

Luke Vincent Samson

A Computer Science student at Bicol University, currently taking an internship at Andale.
