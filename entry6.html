Finishing

Let's go home!

By Luke Vincent Samson

The end of an internship marks not just a conclusion, but a celebration of growth, learning, and the remarkable projects that have been nurtured along the way. Join me as I share the final chapter of my journey as an intern at Andale, a startup transforming the vehicle rental services landscape. From presenting our projects to cherishing the achievements, this experience has been nothing short of unforgettable. The time had come to unveil the culmination of weeks of dedication and collaboration. The team at Andale gathered to witness the presentation of our projects, each a testament to our commitment to innovation. As an intern, this was the moment to showcase the wireframes and UI designs I had meticulously crafted, each bearing the essence of user-centric design and brand identity. Presenting our work was an opportunity to share the insights gained from UX research, the creative processes of UI design, and the iterative journey of wireframing. With enthusiasm, I walked through the thought processes behind each decision, from intuitive navigation to visual hierarchy, all aimed at enhancing the user experience for vehicle rental services. The final day of our internship marked the end of one chapter and the beginning of another. Reflecting on the experiences, the lessons learned, and the relationships forged, a sense of fulfillment permeated the air. The time spent at Andale had not just equipped us with skills, but had immersed us in a culture of curiosity and creativity. The journey as an intern at Andale has been a mosaic of growth, creativity, and camaraderie. From presenting our projects to celebrating achievements, the closing chapters of our internships were a testament to the vibrant culture that drives startups like Andale. As the doors to this chapter close, the windows to the future open, revealing a world of possibilities fueled by the experiences gained and the passion ignited during our time at this remarkable startup.

Author

Luke Vincent Samson

A Computer Science student at Bicol University, currently taking an internship at Andale.
